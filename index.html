Frequently Asked Questions

Use the search box at the top to search by book title or author name. The search is case-insensitive and updates in real-time as you type.

Click the bookmark button (📖) on any book to add it to your bookmarks. Click again to remove it. Your bookmarks are saved automatically.

Click the "Bookmarked" button to filter and show only your bookmarked books. Click "All Books" to return to the full list.

Click the moon icon (🌙) in the top-right corner to toggle dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
